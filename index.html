Our Promise

Honest skincare,
from the start.

Trust is the foundation of DermaKit. Here's exactly how we source, handle, and repack every product we sell.

Verified Sourcing

We purchase exclusively from authorised distributors, verified importers, and licensed pharmacies in Bangladesh. Every batch comes with a receipt and certificate of origin we retain on file. We do not purchase from secondary markets or unofficial sellers.

Hygienic Repacking

All repacking happens in a clean, dry, cosmetic-grade environment using sterile mini containers. Each is sealed and labelled with the original product name, brand, batch number, and expiry date. No relabelling, no deception.

Full Ingredient Disclosure

Each kit insert lists the full INCI ingredient list for every product included, so you can check for allergens before applying anything. You have a right to know exactly what you put on your skin — always.

Batch Traceability

Every kit carries a unique batch code. If you ever doubt a product's authenticity or condition, contact us with the code — we can trace it to the original purchase within 24 hours, guaranteed.

Repacking Steps

How we prepare your kit

01

Product purchase

We buy full-size products from verified sellers only, retaining all purchase records and receipts.

02

Quality check

Each product is inspected for seal integrity, correct texture, smell, and expiry date before repacking.

03

Sterile decanting

Products are transferred into sterilised mini containers using clean, dedicated tools per batch.

04

Label & seal

Each container is labelled with brand, product name, batch ID, and expiry date — then hermetically sealed.
